Who is Crossover?

Crossover, short for Crossover Global is a 501(c)(3) Tax-Exempt Charitable organization. Basedon the teachings of Christ, we aim to provide life assistance to those of our brothers and sisters who need help, where-ever, when-ever, and how-ever they may need it.

What is Crossover's Mission?

We believe that man, meaning every human, "shall not live on bread alone, but on every word that comes from the mouth of God" (Matthew 4:4).

Providing financial assitance to build and re-build the body of Christ. This includes extending financial support to people such as widows, orphans, missionaries, and under privileged individuals and churches.

We provide Aid

We are an organization founded to give and render assitance to the less fortunate members of the Body of Christ- orphans, widows, missionaries, and the underprivileged individuals and churches.

We do this by conducting projects and sponsorships. Proceeds are used to help out those in need, and inividuals, like you, are encouraged to take part of our mission by donating, volunteering, or sponsoring.
